## Owner profile

Primary holder: China Owner — self-use, holiday, or managed rent.

Local JB tenant pool is limited; marketing requires Chinese channels.

Average rent ~RM 2,440; void 8–14 weeks without active management.

## Service fit

Inspection, key-holding and PM revenue align with owner needs.

Rental-only investors should compare yield vs carrying cost carefully.

Golf / marina amenities support lifestyle marketing, not commuter demand.

## Recommendation

Cap FC rental inventory acquisition; prioritize PM relationships.

Price against same-tower recent rents, not JB averages.

Pair listings with Forest City media center for due diligence.

## 业主画像

主要持有人：中国业主——自用、度假或托管出租。

本地新山租客池有限；营销需中文渠道。

均租约 RM 2,440；无活跃管理时空置 8–14 周。

## 服务匹配

巡检、钥匙托管与 PM 收入契合业主需求。

纯租赁投资者需仔细比较收益与持有成本。

高尔夫 / 码头配套支撑生活方式营销，非通勤需求。

## 建议

控制 FC 租赁库存收购；优先 PM 关系。

定价参考同塔近期租金，非新山均价。

房源配森林城市图库便于尽调。
