> CIQ, RTS & Iskandar cross-border rental

Analysis for Singapore-based tenants and investors targeting JB commuter stock — R&F Princess Cove, Medini and selected Mount Austin blocks.

*Published: 2026-06-20*

*Malaysia Property Network — Johor Property Intelligence*

## Commuter corridors

R&F Princess Cove: highest CIQ footfall, RM 2,200–4,500 rent band.

Medini: lower entry, Legoland / Tuas narrative, RM 1,600–3,000.

Weekend and flex-work patterns post-2024 border normalization.

## Pricing drivers

1-bed furnished CIQ view commands premium.

Car park and management responsiveness are top tenant filters.

SGD–MYR spread supports yield arbitrage for investors.

## Risks

Border policy and queue times affect tenant retention.

Oversupply in new Medini phases — price to recent transactions.

# 新加坡通勤住房报告

> 关卡、RTS 与依斯干达跨境租赁

面向新加坡租客与投资者的新山通勤房源分析——富力公主湾、美迪尼及部分奥斯汀山楼栋。

*发布日期: 2026-06-20*

*马来西亚房产网络 — 新山物业研究*

---

## 通勤走廊

富力公主湾：关卡客流最高，租金 RM 2,200–4,500。

美迪尼：入场较低，RM 1,600–3,000。

2024 年后跨境灵活办公与周末居住模式增加。

## 定价因素

一房装修关卡景溢价明显。

车位与管理响应是租客首要筛选。

新马汇率差支撑投资者收益套利。

## 风险

边境政策与排队时间影响租客留存。

美迪尼新盘供应增加——定价需参考近期成交。
